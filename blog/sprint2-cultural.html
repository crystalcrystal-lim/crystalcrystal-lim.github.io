Learning Plan

I hope to remain in the fashion industry and work in the tech/digital sector. I would also like to freelance on the side and develop websites for local brands.


I would like to develop a growth mindset. I want to be able to view failures as opportunities to learn and embrace the process. I believe my current mindset is leaning closer towards a fixed mindset, I often get frustrated and avoid challenges.


My strengths lie in my authenticity and my creativity. I am confident in my design aesthetic and stay true to it.


I am limited by my anxiety and my tendency to allow my emotions to take over. I am easily irritated and highly sensitive, and when I run into problems and challenges I find that I am overcome with negative emotions.

After a few honest attempts and thorough research on the internet, I will let go of my pride and reach out to my cohort and facilitators for help and guidance.


From my facilitation team, I expect open and relaxed communication. I want to feel safe and devoid of judgement when I approach the team with technical and emotional help.
